Hey I’m KESSEY, a DJ based in London. I was born in Oslo but raised in the UK. I’ve toured all over the world and played at some of the biggest festivals including Glastonbury, Cochella, Creamfields and Ultra Music Festival.

I play a mixture of house, techno, drum & bass, electronica, EDM, breakbeat, trance and UK garage. I love a good pop mashup too!

My heroes include Annie Mac, Sister Bliss, Fatboy Slim, Paul Oakenfold, Pete Tong, Avicii and Calvin Harris.

I’m available for hire so get in touch if you’re interested. You can also find me on Soundcloud, YouTube, Facebook and Instagram.
